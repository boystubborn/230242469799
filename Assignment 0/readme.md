##1. Why is the term **data mining** often used to describe the entire knowledge discovery process?

**Knowledge discovery** involves various processes for discovering useful knowledge from large datasets.
_Data mining_ being thought as the core step which applies algorithms to extract patterns from data is sometimes
interchangeably used to mean the whole knowledge discovery process.

##2.differences between data wrangling, scanning and Filtering

**Data Wrangling:** Involves cleaning and transforming raw data into a usable format.

**Data Scanning:** Involves inspecting data to detect patterns, errors, or anomalies before processing.

**Data Filtering:** Involves selecting only the relevant data based on specific conditions.
